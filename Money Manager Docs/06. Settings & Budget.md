## ğŸ§© Part 2: Monthly Budget

### Setting a Budget

**Flow**
1. Home Screen â†’ Monthly Budget card (or Settings â†’ Budget)
2. Tap "Set Budget" or existing amount
3. Enter budget limit in currency
4. Save

**Storage**
- Stored as `MonthlyBudget` entity in Swift Data
- One record per month (year + month)
- If no record exists for month, budget is "unset"

### Budget Display

**On Home Screen**

**Color Indicators**
- 0-50% spent: Green

- 80-100% spent: Red
- Over budget: Dark Red

### Budget Scope

- **Monthly only** (per calendar month)
- **Global** (not per-category)
- Resets automatically on 1st of month

### Edge Cases

- Switching months: Show budget for that month (if set)
- Previous month budget: View in insights (future)
- Delete budget: Revert to "No budget set"
